Dumplings stuffed with meat to the brim

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 egg
  • Â½ cup water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon clarified butter
  • Extra flour for spreading

For the filling:

  • 300 g minced meat
  • 1 onion
  • Salt, pepper
  • 2 tsp melted butter

For the top:

  • Sumac
  • Melted butter
  • Azerbaijani yogurt (garlic to taste)
  1. We add eggs, salt, and water to the sifted flour and knead a normal dough.
  2. We cook and prepare the minced meat in the usual way.
  3. We make two logs from the dough.
  4. We sprinkle a little flour and spread it with an oxlov. The dough should not be too thick or too thin.
  5. We cut circles with a diameter of 4 cm.
  6. We boil the water.
  7. We add 2 teaspoons of salt to the water.
  8. When it starts to boil, we pour the circles.
  9. When it boils, boil for 5 minutes and filter.
  10. Check the salt of the water. Add more if needed.
  11. Place in bowls, pour melted butter on top, and sprinkle with sumac.

Bon Appetit!
