Nutty, tasty kata

Ingredients:

For the dough:

  • 250 g of butter
  • 250 g sour cream
  • 4-4.5 cups flour
  • 1 packet of baking powder
  • 1 package of vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp salt

For filling:

  • 3 bunches of walnut sugar mix

For the face:

  • 1 egg

Preparation:

  1. Pour the flour into the bowl and rub it with oil.
  2. Add sour cream, baking powder, salt, and vanilla. Knead the dough.
  3. Form the dough into a soft ball.
  4. Divide the dough into 3 equal parts.
  5. Roll out each part into an oval shape with a thickness of 0.5 mm.
  6. Sprinkle 1 tablespoon of the filling on top of each oval.
  7. Roll each oval into a tight roll.
  8. Cut each roll into equal parts with a serrated knife.
  9. Place the ready-made buns on a thick sheet of paper.
  10. Brush the tops of the buns with beaten egg.
  11. Bake in an oven preheated to 200 degrees Celsius until golden brown.

Bon Appetit!
