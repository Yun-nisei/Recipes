Hot and ready noodles

Ingredients:

  • 2 servings of cut noodles
  • 2 tsp melted butter
  • 4-5 dried yellow cherries
  • Yellow carrot at the end of C.q
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 6 st meat or chicken broth

Preparation:

  1. Pour the broth into the pot.
  2. When it boils, we add oil, yellow carrot and acid. It boils for about 5 minutes. If the broth is too oily, do not add oil.
  3. Then add the noodles and mix.
  4. When the noodles are cooked, add salt and pepper to taste.
  5. We put the finished dish in bowls and serve it.

Bon Appetit!
