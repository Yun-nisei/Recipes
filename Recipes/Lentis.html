Red hot Lentils soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup red lentils
  • 1 medium onion - finely chopped
  • 1 medium carrot - finely chopped
  • 1 medium potato - cut into small pieces
  • 1 tablespoon tomato (or tomato paste)
  • 6 cups of water
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste (optional)
  • 1-2 tablespoons of oil

For the topping:

  • 2 tablespoons of butter or olive oil
  • 1/3 teaspoon of red sweet pepper powder (paprika)

To serve:

  • Lemon wedges
  • Bread

Preparation:

  1. Boil lentils, onions, potatoes, carrots, tomato, and water in a pot.
  2. Cook uncovered on medium heat for about 20-25 minutes. During this time, if foam appears on the surface of the soup, collect the foam. Stir frequently to prevent burning.
  3. When the ingredients are soft, put them in a blender to make a puree.
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste. Add oil and stir to enhance the flavor.
  5. Pour the soup into bowls. Optionally, heat the oil in a small pan. As it heats up, add the sweet pepper powder to the oil (it should sizzle). Then pour some of this sauce on the soups in the bowl.
  6. Squeeze lemon over the soup and serve with bread.

NOTE 1: This soup thickens as it sits. Add water and mix to adjust consistency.

NOTE 2: If you don't have a blender, you can either mash the food with a potato masher or skip this step. In this case, grate the potatoes and carrots with a large grater so they melt when cooked.

Enjoy!
