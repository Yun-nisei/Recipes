Full and tasty Plov

Ingredients:

  • 5 tbsp of rice
  • Half-cooked meat in 500 g of water
  • 6-7 onions
  • 300 g of cleaned chestnuts
  • 100 g of raisins
  • 100 g of albukhara or cherry pickle
  • 100 g of jam
  • 5-6 tablespoons of melted butter
  • Saffron
  • Yellow ginger
  • Salt
  • Pepper

For qazmaq : You can prepare any type of qazmaq.

Preparation:

  1. We pour boiling water over the saffron and let it brew. Wash the rice and soak it in salted water.
  2. We boil water. Pour salt into the water, cook the rice and filter it.
  3. For qazmaq, you can use any type of qazmaq recipe. Heat a little oil in the pot where we will cook the plov, spread the top, spread it on the bottom of the pot and cut it into shapes.
  4. We pour the filtered rice on top of the rice. We add saffron on top of the rice.
  5. We add oil to the rice and put it on the stove. When we put the plov on the stove to brew, we put a towel on the mouth of the pot. We need to put a thick iron or cast iron pan on the stove. The plov will brew for 1 hour. We need to check in between to make sure that the plov does not burn.
  6. After peeling and washing the onion, cut it lengthwise thinly. Pour salt and oil on top and fry until golden. Add a little yellow carrot and mix.
  7. We sprinkle a little salt on top of the chestnuts and roast them.
  8. We clean and wash the raisins and albukhara and fry them separately in oil.
  9. First, we pour the meat into the pot, then the onion, chestnuts, apricots, pickles and raisins. We sprinkle salt and pepper to taste. We pour a little of the water of the meat and let it cook on high heat.
  10. When it falls into the oil, we take it off the stove.
  11. We collect the knot in the form of a coarse mound and decorate it with qazmaq. (To taste) Then we pour hot oil on top.
  12. We serve the black one next to the rice in another bowl.

Bon Appetit!
