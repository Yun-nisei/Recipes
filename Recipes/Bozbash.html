Meat and potato full Bozbash

Ingredients:

  • kg of mutton cooked in water with bones (beef can also be used)
  • 3 medium sized potatoes
  • 3 medium sized onions
  • 1 tsp tomato
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Coriander
  • Dill

Preparation:

  1. Finely chop the onion.
  2. We pour oil on the onion and fry it.
  3. Add tomato and fry.
  4. When the onion is ready, we divide the potatoes into two parts and fry them with onions.
  5. We pour the meat and fry it a little.
  6. After roasting, we add the meat broth and cook on high heat. When the potatoes are fully cooked, our meal is ready.
  7. We sprinkle finely chopped greens on top of the finished dish.

Bon Appetit!
