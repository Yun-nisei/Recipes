Dovga full of greenery

Ingredients:

  • 3 liters of Azerbaijanese yogurt
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tsp flour (not whole)
  • 1/2 tbsp round rice
  • 4 bunches greens
    • 1 bunch spinach
    • 2 bunches cilantro
    • Less than 1 bunch of dill
    • Less than 1 bunch of mint (if you don't have mint, you can use dry mint too)
  • Salt (to taste)

Note: In Azerbaijani cuisine, different herbs are added to dovga according to taste: spinach, sorrel, kavar, coriander, fennel, mountain coriander, mint, celery, sweet pepper leaf, white beetroot leaf, cincilim, tarpetoyun, garlic leaf, lamb's ear, etc. Due to these greens, the dishes of dovga are different.

Preparation:

  1. After cleaning and washing the greens, chop them into small pieces.
  2. We add washed rice, eggs, flour and a little salt to the yogurt and mix it with a spoon. Yogurt opens well when the setka is beaten with a spoon.
  3. We pour water into the yogurt approximately as much as its own size. We put the prepared buttermilk on the stove. Mix with a wooden spoon.
  4. When the rice is cooked, when the buttermilk begins to boil slowly, we pour the greens and mix.
  5. After pouring the greens, add salt to taste and stir for about 15-20 minutes. (If you want to pour dry mint, then dry mint should be added after pouring the greens)
  6. If you want, you can add a little dry celery to the dovga. Then it will be even more delicious

Bon Appetit!
