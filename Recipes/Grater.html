Grated pie full of jam

Ingredients:

  • 200 g of butter
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 packet of baking powder
  • 1 package of vanilla
  • 3 tbsp flour

For the filling:

  • 200 g of jam

Preparation:

  1. Melt the oil and cool it.
  2. Add the egg and mix.
  3. After adding powdered sugar and mixing, add baking powder, vanilla and flour and mix.
  4. We add flour and knead with our hands until a soft dough is obtained.
  5. We put 1/3 of the dough in the refrigerator and it freezes.
  6. Spread 2/3 in a bowl with paper (the size of the bowl is 35*26 cm).
  7. We spread the jam on top of the dough.
  8. We take out the dough from the refrigerator, rub it on a grater and sprinkle it on top.
  9. We cook at 180-200 degrees for 20-25 minutes.
  10. After cooling, cut and serve.

Bon Appetit!
