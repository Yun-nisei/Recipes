The delicious Baklava

Ingredients:

For the dough:

  1. 1 kg of flour
  2. 3 eggs
  3. 250 g of butter
  4. 1 cup milk
  5. 2 tsp sour cream
  6. 1 tsp dry yeast
  7. Salt

For the filling:

(You can also make the filling with almonds.)

(We remove the shell of the walnut or almond kernel and pass it through a nut grinder)

  1. 1 kg of walnut kernels
  2. 1 kg of powdered sugar
  3. 1/3 teaspoon cardamom

To lubricate:

400 - 450 g of butter

For the syrup:

  1. 2 cups powdered sugar
  2. 1 cup water

For the face:

  1. 2 egg yolks
  2. Almond kernels (you can add hazelnuts or walnut kernels)

Preparation:

  1. Let's prepare the dough. Pour the milk into a bowl and warm it up.
  2. We melt the oil.
  3. Mix the yeast in warm water and let it foam for 5-10 minutes.
  4. We add eggs, yeast, milk, oil, sour cream, and salt to the middle of the flour.
  5. We knead soft dough.
  6. We put nylon on the dough, put a towel, wrap it in a blanket, leave it to ferment for 2 hours.
  7. We prepare the filling. After roasting the walnut in a pan and cleaning the shell, we pass it through a nut smoking machine.
  8. We mix ground walnuts with powdered sugar and cardamom. Melt the butter to grease the tops.
  9. We prepare the syrup. Add water and powdered sugar to the bowl, boil for 2-3 minutes as soon as it boils and remove.
  10. Break the egg yolk into a bowl and beat it to apply it on the face.
  11. We clean the almond kernel for the face.
  12. The dough is baked for 2 hours.
  13. We divide the dough into 12 parts for each pan. We cut two large logs and the rest into small logs.
  14. Spread the top with (a little) flour.
  15. Grease the pan and spread the dough. We pour oil on top and grease it.
  16. Spread 1 cup of filling on each layer.
  17. We spread and grease the top again.
  18. In this way, we prepare all the layers. After putting the last layer, we make the edges well.
  19. We cut the dough in the shape of a rhombus.
  20. We keep it for half an hour. Then we add eggs and almond kernels in the middle.
  21. Bake in an oven heated to 180 degrees until the face is slightly browned. Then we take it out and oil it.
  22. We set the degree to 150 degrees. We take out the face until it is completely red and pour the syrup.
  23. In both cases, we go with the places that are cut with a knife so that both the oil and the syrup spread well.
  24. After pouring the syrup, keep it in the oven for 3-5 minutes and take it out.
  25. When the baklava is warm, we take it out of the pan one by one and put it in another flat dish.

Note: If you want the face of the baklava to be shiny and beautiful, then take 3 tsp of the baklava syrup, melt it with 1 tsp of honey and apply it to the face of the ready hot baklava with a brush.

Bon Appetit!
